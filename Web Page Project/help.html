Redwall Cafe Help
Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs)

  1. Q:Who are you?
    A:We are an old American family that decided to open a cafe in hopes of serving quality food hard-working people. We also hope to set the standard for all cafes across the United States.
  2. Q:How many cafes do you have?
    A:We presently only have one cafe open for business but hope to change that soon!
  3. Q:Where are you located?
    A:Our cozy cafe is located on the corner of 123 Makebelieve St. in downtown Loonyville.
  4. Q:When are you open?
    A:We are open Monday through Friday from 12:00PM to 8:00PM excluding holidays. A more detailed schedule can be found on our Schedule page.
  5. Q:Are you closed on holidays?
    A:We will be closed on any general holidays(I.E. Easter, Thanksgiving, X-mas, etc.). For any other holidays, refer to our Schedule page.
  6. Q:What kind of dishes do you serve?
    A:We serve dishes based on classic Italian recipes that everyone knows and loves. Examples include pizza and spaghetti and meatballs. We also prepare fresh coffee and fountain drinks for a bit of an American touch. See our Products page for more of our dishes.
  7. Q:I'm allergic to _________. Will you alter dishes on request?
    A:If you are allergic to anything in our dishes, we would advise you to not eat them in the first place. However, we will try to cater to your needs within reasonable bounds.
  8. Q:Do you deliver or allow pickup orders?
    A:Yes and yes. We can also delay them if requested.
  9. Q:How far do you deliver?
    A:We deliver within a mile of the cafe. No more than that though.
  10. Q:My delivery order is taking too long!
    A:That's not a question but in any case, our sincere apologies. Loonyville sometimes gets very busy and we only have a few drivers on hand. If you fail to receive your order within an hour, give us a call and it'll be on the house.
